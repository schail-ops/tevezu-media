Questions

Common answers

How long do I have access to my gallery?
Your gallery stays active for 90 days after delivery. If you need more time just reach out and Tevez will extend it for you.
Can I share my gallery link with family?
Yes — just share the gallery name and password with whoever you want to have access. The link works for anyone who has it.
What format are the photos?
All photos are delivered as high resolution JPGs — fully edited, print ready, and optimized for screens too.
What if I can't find my gallery name?
Check the email from Tevez — it includes your gallery name and password. If you can't find it, email [email protected] and he'll resend it.
