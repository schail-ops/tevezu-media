About me

Started with a ball,
stayed for the lens

It started on the sidelines. I was a high schooler with a camera and a basketball team that needed someone to document their season. What was supposed to be a favor turned into a passion — I couldn't put the camera down.

Over the past three years, I've grown from shooting fast-break layups in a dim gymnasium to capturing weddings, portraits, events, lifestyle, and everything in between. Every shoot has taught me something new about light, timing, and what it means to truly see a moment before it disappears.

I'm based in Boston and I bring that same energy I found on that basketball court to every session — focused, intentional, and always chasing the shot that tells the real story.
