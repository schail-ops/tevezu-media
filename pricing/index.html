FAQ

Common questions

How long until I get my photos?
Most sessions are delivered within 5–7 business days. Weddings and large events may take up to 2 weeks. I'll always give you a sneak peek within 48 hours.
Do you travel outside of Boston?
Yes — I'm available for travel throughout New England and beyond. Travel fees may apply depending on distance. Just reach out and we'll figure it out.
Can I customize a package?
Absolutely. If none of the packages fit, send me a message and we can build something that fits your vision and budget.
What if it rains or plans change?
Life happens. I'm flexible on rescheduling as long as we communicate in advance. I'll always work with you to find a new date.
How do I receive my photos?
All photos are delivered through a private online gallery where you can download, share, and order prints. Select packages also include a USB drive.
Do you shoot video too?
Right now I'm focused on photography only, but mixtape packages include highlight reel options. Reach out and let's talk about what you need.
